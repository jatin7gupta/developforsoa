Xerox Document Centre 440

Document Centre 440 integrates paper and digital documents effortlessly, so you can share them quickly and easily around your office or around the world

  • Duty cycle: 200,000 images per month, First Copy Out Time: 3.9 seconds, Rated copy speed: 40 ppm
  • Paper trays can be reloaded while the machine is printing
  • Maximum paper capacity with options: 4,050 sheets, Paper sources: 3 standard including bypass tray, Standard paper capacity: 1,050 sheets, Standard two-sided copying
  • Auto features: paper select, reduction and enlargement, registration, tray switching, job recovery
  • Special features: annotation, cover insertion, build job, booklet creation, bound document copying, sample set, store and recall job programming
  • Trayless duplex - no limit to the number of two-sided output pages
  • Standard Duplex Automatic Document Feeder: 50, Left Catch Tray: 200 sheets, Offsetting Center Tray: 500 sheets, Optional Finisher: Staples up to 50 sheets; 1,000 sheets output capacity
  • Internal Electronic Auditron allows password and account access, Supports up to 2,000 user accounts
